# Staff User Manual: Elvis Salon

## 1. Getting Started
Log in using your provided staff credentials. You will be directed to your **Home** dashboard.

## 2. Managing Requests
- New appointments appear in **Pending Requests**.
- Click **Accept ✓** to move the appointment to your schedule.
- Click **Decline ✕** if you are unavailable; this notifies the admin.

## 3. Viewing Your Schedule
The **Schedule** tab provides a list of all your confirmed clients. You can view their contact details and the specific service they requested.


## 4. Profile Management
Click the circular avatar in the top right to upload a profile picture. Use the **Change Password** button regularly to keep your account secure.
